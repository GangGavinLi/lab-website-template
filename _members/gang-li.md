Dr. Gang Li is an Assistant Professor in the Michael W. Hall School of Mechanical Engineering at Mississippi State University. His research focuses on intelligent energy systems, AI-enabled predictive maintenance, and advanced mechanical design for renewable energy applications.

## Education

- **Ph.D.** in Mechanical Engineering, University of Shanghai for Science and Technology (2017)
- **Visiting Ph.D.**, University of Maryland Baltimore County (2016-2017)
- **M.S.** in Mechanical Engineering, University of Shanghai for Science and Technology (2013)
- **B.S.** in Mechanical Engineering, University of Shanghai for Science and Technology (2010)

## Research Interests

- AI-enabled predictive maintenance for renewable energy systems
- Advanced drivetrain design for tidal and wind energy converters
- Federated learning for distributed energy monitoring
- Time-series forecasting for energy production
- Gear dynamics and mechanical power transmission
- Digital twin technologies for marine systems

We are excited to announce the launch of the Gang Li Research Lab website! This platform will serve as a hub for sharing our research, publications, and updates with the broader community.

Our lab focuses on developing intelligent systems for sustainable energy infrastructure, combining artificial intelligence, machine learning, and advanced mechanical engineering. Through this website, you can:

- Explore our current research projects and publications
- Meet our team of researchers and students
- Learn about opportunities to join or collaborate with us
- Stay updated on our latest achievements and news

We look forward to connecting with students, researchers, and industry partners interested in renewable energy, AI applications, and mechanical systems innovation.

Welcome to our digital home!

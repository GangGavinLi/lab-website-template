Our lab leads and contributes to numerous research projects that advance renewable energy technologies, artificial intelligence applications, and mechanical system design. These projects are supported by federal agencies, industry partners, and institutional resources.

Featured Projects

Transfinity - IVT for Tidal Energy Novel Infinitely Variable Transmission

Developing an innovative infinitely variable transmission system for tidal current energy converters to maximize power capture and improve efficiency. Supported by NSF PFI-TT ($550K).

renewable-energy mechanical-design tidal-energy
Federated Learning for Wind Systems Decentralized Condition Monitoring

Creating privacy-preserving federated learning frameworks for distributed wind system monitoring, enabling collaborative learning without compromising SCADA data security.

artificial-intelligence wind-energy privacy
Wind-to-Hydrogen Forecasting AI-Powered Production Prediction

Developing multivariate CNN-Bi-LSTM models for accurate temporal forecasting of hydrogen production from wind energy systems, enabling better grid integration and storage planning.

Technology Transfer

Several of our technologies are being developed for commercialization:

  • Transfinity IVT: Novel transmission system for tidal energy (NSF I-Corps, TEDCO-MII Grant)
  • ScanMaster: Mirror-assisted continuously scanning laser vibrometry (TEDCO-MII Grant)
  • Windmaster: Improved hybrid vertical axis wind turbine (TEDCO-MII Grant)

These innovations have the potential to significantly impact renewable energy deployment and infrastructure monitoring.

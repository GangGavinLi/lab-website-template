## Research Focus Areas

Our team works on cutting-edge problems at the intersection of:

- **Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning**: Developing federated learning frameworks, predictive maintenance algorithms, and time-series forecasting models
- **Renewable Energy Systems**: Designing advanced drivetrains for tidal and wind energy converters
- **Smart Infrastructure**: Creating condition monitoring systems for distributed energy networks
- **Mechanical Design**: Optimizing gear systems, transmission technologies, and power conversion mechanisms

{% include section.html background="images/background.jpg" dark=true %}

We collaborate with leading institutions and industry partners to translate research into real-world impact. Our graduates go on to careers in academia, national laboratories, and innovative technology companies.
